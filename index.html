Storyline

Marmargee Fighter Girl and her best friend Matmateo Fighter Boy are Astral Undine, Masters of the Vortex.
Zombies and monsters have launched a surprise attack from space devastating the blue planet of Taetania, their homeworld! Luckily Marmargee and Matmateo were able to escape Other worlds are now being invaded by these zombies and monsters and are crying out for help! Will Marmargee be able to stop them? Perhaps with the help of Matmateo?

Goal of the game

In this simple 3-lane runner style game, you can rerun through 10 unique worlds at 3 speed difficulty levels and have fun destroying over 30 different types of Monsters and Zombies while evading over 100 types of obstacles and traps! Replay and increase your overall score and share with your friends or perhaps even with us :-)
